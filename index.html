I am currently a Ph.D. student with the Department of Computing, Imperial College London under the supervision of Dr. Stefanos Zafeiriou and Dr. Bjoern Schuller. During the course of my Ph.D. I worked on deep component analysis techniques for many face related applications. I worked on problems including face clustering, alignment, 3D face reconstruction, and temporal alignment of time-series. In 2016 I was lucky enough to intern with the Google Brain team where I worked on domain adaptation and 3D pose estimation. I am also lucky enough to be a receiver of the prestigious Google Fellowship in Machine Perception, Speech Technology and Computer Vision.
